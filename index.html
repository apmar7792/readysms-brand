05

Usage Guidelines

Quick reference for correct and incorrect brand usage.

Do

Use the gradient (blue-to-cyan) on "Ready" and keep "SMS" in dark or white depending on background.

Do

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "R" around the logo on all sides.

Do

Use Plus Jakarta Sans 700 (Bold) for the wordmark and 800 (ExtraBold) for standalone R marks.

Do

Use the chat bubble icon for app stores, social avatars, and anywhere a square icon is needed.

Don't

Don't italicize "Ready" or any part of the wordmark. The italic treatment has been retired.

Don't

Don't use the gradient on "SMS" — it always stays dark (#0F172A) or white.

Don't

Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds without a container or overlay for legibility.

Don't

Don't stretch, rotate, or add effects (drop shadows, outlines, glows) to the logo.
